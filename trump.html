Biography

Donald John Trump is an American businessman, television personality, and politician. He served as the 45th president of the United States from 2017 to 2021 and as the 47th president beginning in 2025.

Before entering politics, Trump was known for his real estate business, including hotels, golf courses, and commercial properties. He also gained national recognition as the host of the television show The Apprentice.
